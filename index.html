About WCRL

The Wireless Communication Research Lab (WCRL) at IIT Patna, led by Prof. Preetam Kumar in the Department of Electrical Engineering, is committed to advancing the field of next-generation wireless communication systems through both theoretical and experimental research. The lab primarily focuses on addressing open challenges at the physical layer, aiming to enhance communication efficiency, reliability, and adaptability in complex and dynamic wireless environments.


WCRL‚Äôs research covers a broad range of modern technologies, including Reconfigurable Intelligent Surface (RIS)-assisted communication, Index Modulation (IM), Orthogonal Frequency Division Multiplexing (OFDM), and Integrated Sensing and Communication (ISAC). In addition to conventional signal processing techniques, the lab also integrates Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) methods into wireless system design and optimization. These data-driven approaches are applied for tasks such as channel estimation, resource allocation, beamforming, and network adaptation, enabling intelligent and adaptive communication frameworks. The research workflow at WCRL typically begins with mathematical modeling and analytical validation, followed by simulation-based evaluation using statistical and AI/ML-based system models. Once the theoretical models are verified, the team transitions to hardware implementation using platforms like the NI-PXI (National Instruments PXIe) machine for real-time testing and prototyping.


WCRL is actively engaged in several government-sponsored projects and industry collaborations, focusing on technology development and transfer to practical applications. The lab has also filed multiple patents and consistently publishes high-quality research in leading journals and conferences. Through its interdisciplinary work combining signal processing, wireless communication, and AI-driven design, WCRL continues to play a vital role in shaping the future of intelligent and energy-efficient communication systems.

Research Areas

5G and 6G Communications

Advanced research in next-generation wireless communication systems, focusing on massive MIMO, millimeter-wave communications, and network slicing for enhanced mobile broadband and ultra-reliable low-latency communications.

Physical Layer issues in Wireless Communications

Investigation of channel modeling, equalization techniques, interference mitigation, and modulation schemes for improved spectral efficiency and reliability in wireless communication systems.

Signal Processing for Communication Systems

Development of advanced signal processing algorithms for digital communications, including adaptive filtering, beamforming, and optimization techniques for enhanced system performance.

VLSI for Communication

Design and implementation of VLSI architectures for communication systems, focusing on low-power designs, high-speed processing units, and hardware-software co-design methodologies.

Wideband Antenna Design

Research in antenna design for wideband applications, including metamaterial antennas, MIMO antenna systems, and reconfigurable antennas for multi-standard wireless communications.

Underwater Communications

Development of acoustic and optical communication systems for underwater environments, addressing challenges in channel modeling, network protocols, and energy-efficient communication.

About Me

Prof. Preetam Kumar is a distinguished Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at IIT Patna, with over 26 years of experience in academia, research, and industry. His expertise lies in wireless communication systems, including cutting-edge technologies such as 5G/6G, ORAN, and cognitive radios. He has authored over 130 publications in leading journals and conferences, holds three patents, and has collaborated on numerous industry and government-sponsored projects.

His research achievements have been recognized with prestigious awards, including Best Paper Awards at IEEE GLOBECOM 2023 and IEEE ICMOCE 2023. He has guided numerous PhD, MTech, and BTech projects and is currently supervising ten doctoral candidates.

Prof. Kumar has held significant academic leadership roles, including Head of the Department of Electrical Engineering at IIT Patna (2023‚Äì2024) and Member of the Board of Governors (2018‚Äì2021). He has contributed as an editor for Springer‚Äôs Wireless Personal Communications and Wireless Networks journals, and has organized prominent conferences, including serving as General Chair for NCC 2023 at IIT Guwahati.
