Biography

Simmi Perveen is currently a PhD research scholar in the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Patna. She joined the institute in 2020.

Her research interests include reconfigurable intelligent surface (RIS)-assisted full-duplex communication systems, RIS-assisted communication with index modulation, RIS-assisted ambient backscatter communication, and RIS-assisted integrated sensing and communication.
